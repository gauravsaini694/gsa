Tobacco Free Aam Admi Clinics

Dr Balbir Singh, Health Minister, Punjab in a State level event called for declaring all 664 Aam Admi Clinics as Tobacco Free to observe State No Tobacco Day, 2023 on 1st November. No Tobacco Pledge, enforcement drives and sensitization meetings were conducted across all districts in Punjab with the support of the organisation.

Collaborations and Support

GSA is currently supported by Vital Strategies and the World Health Organisation (WHO). Previously, it collaborated with the International Union Against Tuberculosis & Lung Disease (The Union) and Global Health Advocacy Incubator. About Generation Saviour Association

Generation Saviour Association (GSA) is dedicated to public health by leading tobacco control efforts through awareness, enforcement, and community programs. Working across Punjab, Haryana, and Chandigarh, GSA collaborates with policymakers, educators, and health professionals to create a tobacco-free society and promote healthier lifestyles.
