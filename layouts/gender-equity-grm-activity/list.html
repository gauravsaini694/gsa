Gender Equity-GRM activity

"Girls Rise for Mohali," founded by Opinder Preet Kaur in 2015 is a transformative initiative aimed at empowering young girls in the Mohali region and presently expanding in and beyond the region . The program focuses on providing educational opportunities, mentorship, and emotional support to help girls build confidence and self-esteem. It strives to break barriers by encouraging girls to pursue their dreams, whether in academics, sports, or other areas of personal development. By creating a safe space for growth and learning, "Girls Rise" fosters a community where girls can overcome challenges and realize their full potential. Through this initiative, Opinder Preet Kaur is inspiring the next generation of strong, confident women.
