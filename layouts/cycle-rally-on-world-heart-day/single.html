Cycle Rally on World Heart Day

As a part of world heart day celebration on 29th September 2019 a cycle rally was organized by Generation Saviour Association in collaboration with Department of Health and Family Welfare, Punjab to spread the message of awareness regarding keeping heart healthy by physical activity, avoiding unhealthy food (including food containing trans-fat) and abstaining from tobacco and alcohol. The cycle rally was flagged off by Director Health and Family Welfare Punjab Dr. Avneet Kaur. On this occasion other dignitaries Dr. GB Singh SNO NCD, civil surgeon Dr. Manjit Singh, Dr. Kuldeep Singh, Dr. Nidhi, Dr. Harmandeep Kaur Epidemiologist, Dr. Bhupinder Singh, Dr. Hasanpreet Kaur, Dr. Sunaina Bansal, health inspector Bhupinder Singh were also present.
