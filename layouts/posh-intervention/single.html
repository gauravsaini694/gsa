POSH Intervention

Generation Saviour Association also carried out a POSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) intervention, aiming to create safer and more respectful community spaces. The session focused on raising awareness about the provisions of the POSH Act, educating participants on identifying, reporting, and addressing instances of harassment, especially in workplace and community settings. This initiative emphasized the importance of dignity, safety, and gender equality, encouraging proactive measures to foster a culture of respect and accountability.
