World Hypertension Day

On the occasion of World Hypertension Day, Generation Saviour Association (GSA) organized an awareness event dedicated to educating the public about the dangers of high blood pressure. The event was inaugurated by Dr. G. B. Singh, Program Officer, NCD, Department of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Punjab. Under the global theme "Know Your Numbers," the initiative aimed to encourage regular blood pressure screening and improve awareness among all age groups. The event witnessed active participation from citizens eager to learn about hypertension prevention and management. Through such initiatives, GSA continues to aimed to raise awareness about blood pressure management and promote healthier lifestyles.
