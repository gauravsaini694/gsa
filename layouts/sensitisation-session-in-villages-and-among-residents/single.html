Sensitisation Session in Villages and among Residents

Generation Saviour Association conducted a sensitization session in Village Siau, Mohali, Tobacco Free Residential complex with residents of Jal Vayu Enclave focusing on the ill effects of tobacco use. The session aimed to educate residents about the health risks associated with tobacco and promote awareness about tobacco control laws. Community members actively participated, showing keen interest in adopting healthier lifestyles and supporting tobacco-free initiatives at the village level.
