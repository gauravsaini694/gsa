Stay Away from Drugs: Stay Safe from HIV AIDS

To raise awareness about AIDS and substance abuse Generation Saviour Association conducted a sensitization session for students and staff of Chitkara University where Ms Opinder Preet Kaur Gill,Director GSA and Dr Rakesh Gupta,President, SIPHER shared their views with students. Students also participated in Art competition conducted by GSA where they shared art forms like videos, painting, posters, short stories on AIDS awareness. Students also took pledge for abstaining from substance abuse including tobacco.
