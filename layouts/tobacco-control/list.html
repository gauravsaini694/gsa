Tobacco control

Generation Saviour Association (GSA) is dedicated to safeguarding public health through tobacco control, non-communicable disease (NCD) prevention, and community awareness programs. We collaborate with law enforcement, health professionals, and educational institutions to promote healthier environments. Our efforts include supporting the National Tobacco Control Program (NTCP) through awareness and enforcement drives, organizing health camps, and fostering youth engagement for long-term behavioural change.

GSA leads initiatives such as Tobacco-Free Buildings, Children Against Tobacco, Healthy School Campaign, and Courtesy Notices to Tobacco Vendors to create lasting community impact. By working closely with schools, policymakers, and local vendors, we ensure a collective approach to public health advocacy.
