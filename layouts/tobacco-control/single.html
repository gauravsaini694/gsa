Tobacco control

GSA aims to protect present and future generations from tobacco which is the risk factor for many Non-Communicable Diseases, and also from social, environmental & economic scourges of tobacco and aware of them for the best health potential. The Organization works closely with the World Health Organization, The Union against Tuberculosis and Lung Diseases and the Department of Health and Family Welfare, Punjab, National Health Mission, Haryana and Chandigarh.
