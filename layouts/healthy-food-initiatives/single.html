Healthy Food Initiatives

Generation Savior Association has launched the Healthy Food Campaign to eliminate trans-fat foods from school canteens and surrounding areas within a 200-meter radius of schools. The campaign urges schools to ban the sale of junk food and encourages parents to pack healthy, trans-fat-free tiffins for their children. With rising cases of cardiovascular diseases in Punjab, this initiative aims to create healthier school environments and promote better eating habits among students. President Opinder Kaur Gill emphasizes the urgent need for parents and schools to work together to safeguard the health of the next generation.
