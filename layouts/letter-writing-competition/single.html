Letter Writing Competition

On 8th November 2019, Generation Saviour Association organized a letter writing competition addressed to the Prime Minister, centered on the 'Eat Right Movement'. Before the competition, Ms. Opinder Preet Kaur Gill, President of GSA, presented on the dangers of misleading advertisements promoting unhealthy foods, especially targeting young audiences through television and social media. The presentations also highlighted the health risks associated with trans-fat consumption.Students from various schools in Mohali participated enthusiastically. The session was highly interactive, and the top four letters will be awarded and sent to the be awarded and sent to the Prime Minister
