About Generation Saviour Association

The Beginning

Late Ms. Amteshwer Kaur, a practicing advocate in Punjab and Haryana High Court filed her first case, a public Interest Litigation, to ban smoking at public places in the year 1996, marking the birth of Generation Saviour Association (GSA), which was registered in 1997. With her tireless efforts and far sightedness, an army against smoking and tobacco use grew stronger with addition of various patrons from fields like Education, Health, Police, Administration and Judiciary. The founder’s vision and self-less efforts, brought her laurels from both the district and the State.

Director Generation Saviour Association

Mr Amarjeet Singh

Presently GSA is headed by Sardar Amarjeet Singh, who with his unwavering vision for the organisation and over three decades of experience in finance and management is dedicated to social change and community development. He is a timeless cornerstone of GSA embodying wisdom, experience, and unwavering commitment, steering organisation's strategic direction, fostering partnerships, and ensuring accountability. He has always been the torchbearer and the taskmaster for the organization since it’s inception.

Opinder Preet Kaur Gill
