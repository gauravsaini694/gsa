Our Mission and Vision

Generation Saviour Association (GSA) is committed to protecting public health by reducing tobacco use, preventing non-communicable diseases (NCDs), and promoting healthier lifestyles. Through advocacy, education, and capacity building, policy initiatives, strategic partnerships, we aim to create a tobacco-free society and empower communities to make informed health choices.

Our mission extends beyond tobacco control to address emerging health threats, including child obesity, cardiovascular diseases, and hypertension. By engaging youth, collaborating with policymakers, and working at the grassroots level in Punjab, Haryana, and Chandigarh, we strive to build a healthier future for generations to come.
