Regional Workshop on Promotion, Adoption & implementation

Implementation of New Initiatives for Tobacco Control Representatives from 9 states participated in Regional Workshop on Promotion, Adoption and Implementation of New Initiatives for Tobacco Control organised by Generation Saviour Association held under leadership of Dr Balbir Singh, Health Minister, Punjab.The Key recommendations from the Regional consultation were- Encouraging and Institutionalising Tobacco-free Jurisdictions, Mandatory License for Selling Tobacco ,Increasing tobacco access age at par with alcohol permissible age, Effective E -Cigarette Ban, Prohibiting government jobs to tobacco users, Joint enforcement squad, Effective and comprehensive ban on TAPS, Harnessing the power of social media and OTT platforms for effective tobacco control, Increasing access to tobacco-cessation treatment and products and Integration with other programmes.
