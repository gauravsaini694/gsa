DLCC- District Level Coordination Committee Meeting

The organisation, in collaboration with the District Tobacco Control Cells, conducted District Level Coordination Committee (DLCC) meetings under the chairpersonship of the Deputy Commissioners in various districts of Punjab and Haryana. These meetings aimed to strengthen interdepartmental coordination, review the implementation of tobacco control measures under the National Tobacco Control Programme (NTCP), and reinforce compliance with the Cigarettes and Other Tobacco Products Act (COTPA) More than 500 district-level stakeholders were sensitized by the organization in presence of Deputy Commissioners
