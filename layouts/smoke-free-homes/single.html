Smoke-free homes

Generation Saviour Association started its new chapter 'Tobacco Smoke Free Homes' at House Building Cooperative Society, Sector-79, Mohali, Punjab. The association organized a sensitisation session for the residents of the society to raise awareness about the harmful effects of tobacco. Members of the Cooperative House Building Society were also present on the occasion. Ms. Opinder Preet Kaur, President of GSA, Mr. Hakam Singh Jawandha, Media Advisor, GSA, Mr. Ashoke Sofat, President, Cooperate House Building Society, Mohan Lal Lot, Vice President, Cooperate House Building Society, Mr. Suresh Kumar Sharma and Mr. Adeyatam Kumar, Executive Members, Mr. Briginder Singh, Advocate, Mr. Vipin Bajaj, Member GSA discussed the issue with the audience and appeal to them to declare their houses as 'Tobacco Smoke Free Homes'
