Girls Rise celebrated International Women Day in collaboration with Khalsa College of Technology and Business Studies, Mohali in 2021

Esteemed speakers from various walks of life exchanged their views of numerous aspects of gender equality, from politics to business from theatre to art. Women of all age groups came forward and shared their experiences and raised their voice for the concern of equality, without gender bias. Women discussed the reasons and possible solutions to create a gender neutral society, where females are not looked down upon and where females are given opportunities to rise, shine and achieve.
