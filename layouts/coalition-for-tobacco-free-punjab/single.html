Coalition for Tobacco Free Punjab

Coalition for Tobacco Free Punjab was inaugurated and established by GSA. The membership is extended to Non-Government Organisations across Punjab working in areas like Health, Child Rights, Women Empowerment, Education and the like. The coalition aims to work towards establishing a Tobacco Free Punjab.
