Orientation , Capacity Building Workshop under NTCP , Chandigarh

Generation Saviour Organization technically supported Department of Health and Family Welfare, Chandigarh for Orientation & Capacity Building Workshop under National Tobacco Control Programme (NTCP) for Stakeholder Departments & Health Care Professionals under Chairmanship of Sh Yashpal Garg, IAS, Health Secretary Chandigarh . More than 100 stakeholders were sensitized from various stakeholder departments about National Tobacco Control Program and their role under the same.
