State Level Coordination Committee Meeting,Punjab

SLCC meeting was held in Punjab in February 2024 under the Chairmanship of Sh Ajoy Sharma, IAS, Health Secretary where the representatives from 16 departments while reviewing the NTCP program discussed the need and necessity of Tobacco Vendor Licensing in Punjab and how it will support COTPA, 2003 implementation.
