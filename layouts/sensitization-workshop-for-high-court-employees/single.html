Sensitization Workshop for High Court Employees

Generation Saviour Association organized a Sensitization Workshop for the employees of the High Court with the objective of raising awareness about the serious health risks associated with tobacco consumption and exposure to second-hand smoke. During the session, employees were informed about the harmful impact of tobacco not just on users, but also on non-smokers through passive smoking. They were sensitized to the urgent need for creating a healthier and safer workplace environment. As part of the workshop, all participants were encouraged to take collective action towards making the High Court premises a Tobacco-Free Zone, in line with national tobacco control laws and public health goals. The initiative aimed to foster a culture of wellness and responsibility among the High Court workforce, inspiring them to become champions for a tobacco-free society.
