Community Level Activity

Generation Saviour Association (GSA), Mohali, has actively promoted a tobacco-free generation at the community level through various initiatives. These include the Tri-State Consultation on Tobacco Vendor Licensing, sensitization sessions on AIDS and substance abuse, and POSH Act workshops to create safer, respectful environments. GSA also organizes an annual World No Tobacco Day painting competition, engaging over 1,000 students, and conducts workshops for tobacco vendors across Punjab, Haryana, and Chandigarh to raise awareness and ensure compliance with tobacco laws.
