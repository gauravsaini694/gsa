Healthy School Initiative

The Healthy Schools Initiative helps schools combat Non-Communicable Diseases (NCDs) through teacher training, student activities, and health education. By promoting NCD awareness and prevention, we create a wellness culture and teach future generations healthy habits. Supported by the World Health Organization (WHO), this program offers expert guidance and resources for lasting success.
