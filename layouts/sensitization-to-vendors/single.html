Sensitization to vendors

A Sensitization workshop for tobacco retailers, vendors, shopkeepers, and wholesalers was organized to educate them about the tobacco law by Generation Saviour Association under the chairpersonship of the District Nodal Officer, with the support of the District Tobacco Control Cell, covering the tri-state of Punjab, Chandigarh, and Haryana.
