Annual Painting Competition

Every year GSA organizes an Annual Painting Competition to observe World No Tobacco Day, with participation from 1000-1500 students from the tricity area. An exhibition and a signature campaign are also held on that day to raise awareness about the effects of tobacco and encourage students to live a tobacco-free life.
