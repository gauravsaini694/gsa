Area of Work and Mission

Generation Saviour Association in its successful existence of more than two decades has been conducting various activities in Punjab, Haryana and Chandigarh with the support of State and District administration. The Association, as the name suggests, intensively works towards saving the present and the future generation from the increasing dangers arising out of Public Health Issues.

GSA aims to prevent present and future generations as well as the socio-economic environment from public health issues and challenges in general and Non - Communicable Diseases in particular; through awareness, capacity building, sensitization, policy initiatives, collaborations and coordination. At GSA, we believe that Each life matters. Saving a life is saving the entire world

The scope of activities includes Tobacco Control, Controlling Child Obesity, Trans fat elimination, preventing cardio vascular diseases and hypertension and protecting against other Non-Communicable diseases, Fostering Healthy Lifestyle and other emerging public health threats.

Generation Saviour Association diligently works on Youth Intervention Programmes and also has presence at grass root levels in Punjab, Haryana and Chandigarh. Generation Saviour Association regularly conducts awareness campaigns, trainings of stakeholders, interactive sessions and other events, time to time rural setting in villages across Punjab, Haryana and UT Chandigarh with the support of local administration.
