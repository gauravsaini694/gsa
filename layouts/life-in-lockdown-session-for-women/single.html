Let's Talk Life in Lockdown

GSA held an online session on Let's Talk Life in Lockdown on April 24, 2020,

Lockdown was not been easy, especially for Women!. Juggling between work from Home and Managing Home was exhausting. It not only impacted the physical health but also the mental well being of women, leading a lasting impact, hampering their personal professional and wellbeing.

Therefore a special online session was held by Girls Rise where women from all age groups were called to share their experiences from the lockdown due to COVID-19. Experts shared ways to deal with the lockdown and how to fight against the tough time. Women shared their stories of struggles and victories
