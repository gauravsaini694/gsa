Awareness Program for Health care communities

An awareness program was conducted at Dr. B. R. Ambedkar State Institute of Medical Sciences (AIMS), Ajitgarh by Generation Saviour Association in collaboration with the Legal Services Authorities, Mohali. The session aimed to educate medical students and faculty on important health and legal issues, including tobacco control, patients’ rights, and the role of legal awareness in promoting public health. This initiative was part of GSA’s ongoing efforts to build informed and responsible healthcare communities.
