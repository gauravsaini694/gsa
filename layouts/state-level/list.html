State level

At the state level, Generation Saviour Association provides technical support for State Level Coordination Committee (SLCC) meetings under the National Tobacco Control Programme (NTCP). These meetings bring together key departments to review progress, discuss Tobacco Vendor Licensing, and strengthen the implementation of COTPA, 2003 and related tobacco control measures.
