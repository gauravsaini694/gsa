Sensitization Workshop

Sensitization Workshop of Police Department for police officials from District Panchkula,Yamunanagar, and Ambala was held with the technical support of GSA where they were sensitized about the National Tobacco Control Programme and their role in tobacco control in. The workshop was held under the Chairmanship of Mr. Sumer Pratap Singh, ACP, Panchkula, and Dr. Rita Kotwal, State Nodal Officer, NTCP, Haryana. More than 100 Police Officials were sensitized during the session.
