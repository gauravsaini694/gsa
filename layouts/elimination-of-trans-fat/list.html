Elimination of Trans Fat

Industrially-produced trans fat is responsible for 540,000 deaths globally each year, besides causing hypertension and cardio vascular diseases. Apart from pushing on strict and comprehensive implementation of Tras fat Regulations under FSSA, we at GSA also promote Strategical Behavioral Change Approach and also work towards easy availability of alternative healthy food options.
